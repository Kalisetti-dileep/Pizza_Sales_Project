# Pizza_Sales_Project
![image alt](https://github.com/Kalisetti-dileep/Pizza_Sales_Project/blob/363f4a0730144d4ad19cb0bb43a3314c1a6d84ff/project%20image.png)
Objective: Create an interactive and visually engaging dashboard to analyze and present key sales metrics for a pizza restaurant. The dashboard is designed using Excel (Power Query and Charts) to help the restaurantâ€™s management gain insights into their sales performance, busiest periods, top-performing items, and other critical aspects of their business.

Data Source: A spreadsheet file containing order and sales data, including details on pizza categories, sizes, order dates, and times.

Dashboard Components:

KPI Metrics:

Total Revenue: Displays the total revenue generated from pizza sales.
Average Order Value: Shows the average value of an order.
Total Pizzas Sold: Indicates the total number of pizzas sold.
Total Orders: Number of individual orders placed.
Average Pizzas Per Order: Provides the average count of pizzas per order.
Busiest Days and Times:

Days: Highlights the days with the highest order volumes, particularly weekends (Friday and Saturday).
Times: Shows peak hours for orders, especially between 12:00-1:00 PM and 4:00-8:00 PM.
Daily Trend for Total Orders:

Bar chart displaying the distribution of total orders across each day of the week.
Hourly Trend for Total Orders:

Line chart illustrating the hourly distribution of orders throughout the day, helping identify peak ordering hours.
Sales by Category & Size:

Breakdown of sales by pizza category (Classic, Supreme, Veggie, Chicken) and by size (Large, Medium, Regular, X-Large, XX-Large), highlighting which combinations contribute most to total sales.
% of Sales by Pizza Category and Size:

Donut chart and pie chart showing the percentage distribution of sales by pizza category and size, providing insights into the most popular categories and sizes among customers.
Top 5 Best and Worst Sellers:

Best Sellers: Lists the top 5 pizzas with the highest sales.
Worst Sellers: Lists the bottom 5 pizzas with the lowest sales, helping in inventory and menu management.
Total Pizzas Sold by Pizza Category:

Horizontal bar chart showing total sales by pizza category, with color-coding for easy comparison.
Timeline Slicer / Filter:

A timeline filter allowing users to select specific months or periods to analyze seasonal sales trends.
Key Insights:

The dashboard provides insights into peak sales periods, high-performing products, and customer preferences by category and size.
Management can make data-driven decisions to optimize staffing, inventory, and promotions based on identified trends.
Tools and Techniques:

Excel Power Query: To clean, transform, and load data for analysis.
Excel Charts: To visualize key metrics and trends, including bar charts, line charts, pie charts, and donut charts.
Timeline Filter: For dynamic filtering by date to view historical performance.
